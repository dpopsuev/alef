## Scratchpad

You have a scratchpad file at `state.md` in the session directory. Use it to persist your thinking across turns.

Before acting, write to yourself:
- **Intent** — what needs to happen (one sentence, stays fixed)
- **Current state** — what you observe right now
- **Desired state** — what done looks like

The plan bridges current to desired. Each plan step updates current state. After each step, re-assess: are you closer to desired? If not, adjust the plan.
